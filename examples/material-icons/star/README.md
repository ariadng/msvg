# star

An animated star icon (msvg package).

- **Intro:** the star spins and scales in with a soft overshoot.
- **loadingLoop:** a twinkle — a slight rotate wobble paired with a scale shimmer and an `opacity 1 -> 0.85 -> 1` dip, looping seamlessly (first and last keyframes are identical: `rotate(0deg) scale(1)` at full opacity).
- **hover:** a quick sparkle wobble.

Icon from Google Material Symbols (Apache-2.0).
