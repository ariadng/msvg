# wechat

An animated WeChat logo (msvg package).

- **intro:** the mark rises and fades in softly (~480ms).
- **loadingLoop:** a chat-bubble float — a gentle `translateY` bob of about 1.5px up and back down with a soft ease, looping seamlessly (first and last keyframes both `translateY(0)`).
- **hover:** a single gentle lift.

Icon from Simple Icons (CC0). WeChat and its logo are trademarks of their respective owner.
