# whatsapp

An animated WhatsApp logo (msvg package).

- **intro:** the mark fades and scales in with a soft overshoot.
- **loadingLoop:** a ringing wiggle — quick rotate swings of `-8` / `+8` / `-5` / `+5` degrees, then a rest period, looping seamlessly (first and last keyframes both `rotate(0deg)`).
- **hover:** a single ring-and-settle wiggle.

Icon from Simple Icons (CC0). WhatsApp and its logo are trademarks of their respective owner.
