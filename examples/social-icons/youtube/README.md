# youtube

An animated YouTube logo (msvg package).

- **intro:** the mark fades and pops in with a soft overshoot.
- **loadingLoop:** a play-button press — `scale(1)` dips to `0.94`, springs back past `1`, settles, then rests, looping seamlessly (first and last keyframes both `scale(1)`).
- **hover:** a single press-and-release on tap.

Icon from Simple Icons (CC0). YouTube and its logo are trademarks of their respective owner.
