# telegram

An animated Telegram logo (msvg package).

- **intro:** the plane flies in from the lower-left and levels off (~520ms).
- **loadingLoop:** a paper-plane glide — drifts diagonally to `translate(2px, -2px)` with a slight rotate lean, then eases back to start, looping seamlessly (first and last keyframes both `translate(0,0) rotate(0)`).
- **hover:** a single diagonal glide and return.

Icon from Simple Icons (CC0). Telegram and its logo are trademarks of their respective owner.
