# instagram

An animated Instagram logo (msvg package).

- **intro:** the mark fades in with a small unwinding twist.
- **loadingLoop:** a shutter wink — a slight rotate wobble paired with a brief opacity dip, like a camera taking a shot, then a rest, looping seamlessly (first and last keyframes both `rotate(0deg)`, `opacity 1`).
- **hover:** a single shutter wink.

Icon from Simple Icons (CC0). Instagram and its logo are trademarks of their respective owner.
